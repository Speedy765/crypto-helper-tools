• FAQ

    How does CryptoTracky gather information

    CryptoTracky gathers information through the Bittrex.com Exchange API and transforms this for optimal use.

  • How does CryptoTracky store information

    At this point it only stores which coins you chose to ignore in your browser, nothing else is saved.

  • Does CryptoTracky also work with other exchanges besides Bittrex.com

    Currently CryptoTracky is based solely on the information on Bittrex.com further development will continue to investigate other options and Exchanges.

  • How can I donate

    Used the address found on most pages, LTC is the coin because the transaction fees are pretty low compared to BTC. If you would like to donate $25 spending $10 on fees is a bit crazy. Still want to use a different currency just let me know.

  • Why do you list a price per second?

    Listing a price per second in real-time! gives you the opportunity to see what is really going on. The information on Bittrex listed in the candlestick charts is delayed and sometimes not accurate. It has happened that a buy or sell order was executed while in the charts a CryptoCurrency never hit the exact number. This happens because the candlestick chart's interval can only be set to a minute as the shortest time interval, never ever really displaying the maximum or minimum price of the CryptoCurrency at that time.
