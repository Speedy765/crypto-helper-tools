Overview

On the left side you will see timeframes starting from 1 minute, 5 minutes, 15 minutes, 30 minutes, 60 minutes all the way up to 240 minutes. Each table displays the coins who gain the most in price. At the end of these columns you will find a "Real-time" column.

Again it is possible to open the real-time price per second page of each coin on the column on the far right. The column of the far left which states "Coin” will bring you directly to the designated page on the active exchange.

On the right side of the overview page you will find an entire list of CryptoCurrency that is changing price or volume. The columns over here can be sorted by clicking on the column header. This way they can be easily sorted on "Price increase”, total "Volume” or "Volume Increase”.

Realtime

Once you click "Open” on the "Real-time” column it will bring you to a real-time price per second overview with the following colored lines:

Top: Asking price

Bottom: Bid price

This provides a lot more information than the ~5min delay on Bittrex. It can be used for trading with minutes or jump on a moon and get out before it drops hard again.
